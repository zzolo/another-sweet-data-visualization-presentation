Data visualization is __________.

* It is just what it sounds like.
* It's really any visual representation of data or information.
* It's charts, graphs, maps, and other design techniques.
* It's functional art, like furniture.
* It serves a practical purpose. Like sitting.
* But it needs to be more than just practical. * It needs to be comfortable and easy to use.
* And/or serve a specific need.
* Almost most importantly, it needs to look good.
